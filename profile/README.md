# Astraform

**Customer outcome simulation for AI-enabled business systems.**

Most AI evals test the agent. **Astraform tests the business outcome.**

Astraform helps teams run synthetic customer cohorts through existing agents, APIs, tools, policies, and workflows before production. The goal is simple: reveal customer impact, policy failures, escalation failures, risky automation behavior, and operational bottlenecks before real customers experience them.

## What Astraform Tests

- **Customer cohorts** with goals, constraints, risk profiles, and success criteria.
- **Business scenarios** such as hardship handling, disputes, recommendations, escalation thresholds, and tool/API access changes.
- **Systems under test** including AI agents, APIs, MCP tools, A2A workflows, policies, and operational rules.
- **Outcome reports** that compare baseline versus proposed decisions.
- **Replay evidence** tied to customer profiles, timelines, tool calls, policy checks, state changes, and failure reasons.

## Current Wedge

The first proof is a banking customer wind tunnel: synthetic customers experience AI-enabled banking workflows over simulated time, and Astraform reports whether a proposed change is safe enough to launch.

## For Domain Engineers

Astraform supports a bring-your-own-business-world path through `remote-domain.v1`. Domain teams own business state, actions, and evidence projections; Astraform owns simulation time, replay, guardrails, and outcome evidence.
